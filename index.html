About us



Established in Columbus, OH, Melanie’s Business Consulting provides in person and online business consulting services Nationwide.

With over 5 years of experience, Melanie’s Business Consulting has contributed to the growth of more than 50 small businesses, providing turn-key support services for establishing a business as well as financial, marketing and commercial solutions.

Our main goal is helping our clients make distinctive, lasting, and substantial improvements in their performance and to build a great businessthat attracts, develops and grows with no limit.

Additionally:
· Achieve sustainable growth and long-term success, growing small operations into resilient and prosperous ventures.
· Optimize operations, expand market share, enhance profitability, and overcome obstacles.

Melanie’s Business Consulting understands how the elements of transformation fit together and how you can elevate your business to the next level!

Solutions

That will help you plan, start, develop and grow your business. Count on us for this excited journey.

Establishing a Business

  • Business Planning: developing a company's mission, benchmarks, short, long term goals and defining the strategies, tasks and resources needed to achieve them.
  • EIN (Employer Identification Number)
  • Business Registration, Dissolution or Updates (agent/member or address)
  • Business Insurances: General Liability and Workers Compensation
  • LLC Operating Agreement
  • Government Forms (W-9, 1099)
  • Business Contracting: Subcontractors Agreement, Service/Client Agreement and NDA Agreement.
  • Licenses

Marketing

  • Marketing Planning: identifying your target audience, the most effective channels on which to engage with them, and analytical insights to guide future strategy.
  • Visual Identity & Branding: Logo, slogan, graphic design (colors, elements, fonts).
  • Websites and Domain Registration
  • Social Media Management: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Yelp, Google My Business.
  • Google Ads Campaigns and Ad Conversion Tracking
  • Marketing Supplies and Print Advertising: Business cards, Flyers, Banners, Signs, Doors Hangers, Postcards, etc.
  • Professional Uniforms

Commercial & Sales

  • Implementing Estimate, Invoice and Contract Software
  • Commercial Meetings: virtual and in-person
  • Filling Commercial Applications and Forms
  • Filling Digital Marketplace Apps Profile: Thumbtack, Angilist, Bark, HomeAdvisor, Craigslist.
  • Preparing Commercial Presentations
  • Preparing Sales Agreement or Client / Service Contract

Financial & Accounting

  • Business Financial Planning
  • Financial Management: cashflow, bank reconciliation, accounts payable and accounts receivable controlling, cost vs budget analysis (by project).
  • Financial Reports: monthly, quarterly and annually analysis of results.
  • Profit and Loss Statements

Personal Consulting

  • Opening Checking and Savings bank account.
  • Translator & Interpreter: Doctor appointment, court, dealers, etc.
  • File Applications: Hospital expenses, credit card, house/apt leasing, etc.
  • Start or Transfer Utilities services: Water, gas, internet, etc.
